How To Calculate   Hide

Let the Length of the edge of the Cube be \(L\) units.

Then, the Volume of the Cube will be \(L^3\) cubic units.

Let the Length, Breadth and Height of the Cuboid be \(L, B, H\) units respectively.

Then, the Volume of the Cuboid will be \(L.B.H\) cubic units.

Let the Radius of the Cone be \(R\) units

and the Height of the Cone be \(H\) units.

Then, the Volume of the Cone will be \(1/3 \pi R^2 H\) cubic units.

Let the Radius of the Cylinder be \(R\) units

and the Height of the Cylinder be \(H\) units.

Then, the Volume of the Cylinder will be \(\pi R^2 H\) cubic units.

Let the Radius of the Sphere be \(R\) units.

Then, the Volume of the Sphere will be \(4/3 \pi R^3\) cubic units.

Let the Radius of the Hemisphere be \(R\) units.

Then, the Volume of the Hemisphere will be \(2/3 \pi R^3\) cubic units.

Let the Smaller Radius of the Conical Frustum be \(r\) units,

the Larger Radius of the Conical Frustum be \(R\) units

and the Height of the Conical Frustum be \(H\) units,

Then, the Volume of the Conical Frustum will be \(2/3 \pi R^3\) cubic units.
