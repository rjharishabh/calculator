Then

the Volume of the Cube will be \(L^3\) cubic units.

the Total Surface Area of the Cube will be \(6L^2\) square units.

the Lateral Surface Area of the Cube will be \(4L^2\) square units.

the Length of Face Diagonal of the Cube will be \(\sqrt{2}L\) units.

the Length of Space Diagonal of the Cube will be \(\sqrt{3}L\) units.
