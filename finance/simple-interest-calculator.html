How To Calculate Simple Interest  Hide

Let the Radius the Circle be \(R\) units.

Then

the Diameter of the Circle will be \(2R\) units.

the Circumference of the Circle will be \(2\pi R\) units.

the Area of the Circle will be \(\pi R^2\) square units.
