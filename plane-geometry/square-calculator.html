Lets first understand, what is a square and how we can find different properties of a square?

Square

A square is a regular quadrilateral, means that it has four equal sides and four equal angles.

Square can also be defined as a rectangle in which two adjacent sides have equal length.

Properties of a square

1. The diagonals of a square bisect each other and meet at 90°.

2. The diagonals of a square bisect its angles.

3. All four sides of a square are equal.

4. The diagonals of a square are equal.

5. All four angles of a square are equal.

6. Opposite sides of a square are both parallel and equal in length.

Formulas

Let the Side Length of the Square be \(L\) units.

Diagonal of the Square will be \(\sqrt{2}L\) units.

Perimeter & area of a square

Perimeter of the Square will be \(4L\) units.

Area of the Square will be \(L^2\) square units.

For calculating different properties of the square using above square calculator, you have to just write the values in the given input boxes and press the calculate button, you will get the result.
